# 🏦 Bank Marketing Campaign Dashboard — IBM Cognos Analytics

## Project Overview
An interactive IBM Cognos Analytics dashboard analyzing a real-world 
bank telemarketing campaign dataset containing 11,162 customer records. 
This dashboard provides a comprehensive visual overview of customer 
conversion patterns, demographic segmentation, and campaign performance 
to support data-driven marketing strategy decisions.

## Tools Used
- IBM Cognos Analytics (Trial)
- Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset (Kaggle)

## Dataset
- **Source:** Kaggle — Bank Marketing Campaign Dataset
- **Rows:** 11,162 customer records
- **Fields:** Age, Job, Education, Balance, Contact Method,
  Month, Campaign, Previous Outcome, Term Deposit Subscription

## Dashboard Features

| Visual | Insight |
|---|---|
| KPI — Total Customers | 11.2K customers contacted |
| KPI — Total Subscribed | 5.29K customers subscribed |
| KPI — Conversion Rate | 47% overall conversion rate |
| KPI — Average Balance | 1.53K average account balance |
| Bar Chart — Subscribed by Job | Students lead at 74.72% conversion |
| Bar Chart — Subscribed by Age Group | 18-24 highest at 73.4% |
| Line Chart — Subscribed by Month | Monthly campaign performance trend |
| Donut Chart — Subscribed by Deposit | Yes vs No subscription split |
| Interactive Filter — Month | Filter all visuals by month |
| Interactive Filter — Job | Filter all visuals by job type |

## Key Business Insights

- **Students convert at 74.72%** — nearly double the overall 
  average of 47%. Bank should consider targeted student 
  deposit products and campus outreach campaigns.

- **18-24 age group shows 73.4% conversion** — young professionals 
  just starting their financial journey are highly receptive 
  to term deposit products.

- **47% overall conversion rate** across 11,162 contacts — 
  strong campaign performance with clear demographic 
  segments showing significantly higher potential.

- **Average account balance of 1.53K** — subscribers hold 
  41% higher average balance than non-subscribers, confirming 
  financial capacity as a key predictor of deposit decisions.

- **Interactive filters** allow stakeholders to drill down 
  by job type and month — enabling dynamic, self-service 
  data exploration without technical knowledge.
